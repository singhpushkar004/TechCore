// OUR MISSION

Empowering Engineers
of Tomorrow

Founded in 2017 by a team of IIT engineers and robotics enthusiasts, TechCore was born out of a simple frustration — quality components and real-world tech education were hard to find in India.

We set out to change that. Today we serve over 50,000 engineers, students, and hobbyists across India with genuine components, expert services, and courses taught by working professionals.
